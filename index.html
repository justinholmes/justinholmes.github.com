Virgin Media BH9 Bournemouth Oversubscribed.

I am really fed up with Virgin Media’s service in the BH9 area. They have oversubscribed the service and thus have a local upstream utilisation issue on the chassis (UBR).

It is unusable during 12pm and 12am.

It is soo bad that my terminal ssh connections suffer from Write failed: Broken pipe within a minute.

Streaming is also a non-go area, YouTube does not play videos.

Each time a fix has been promised, first for us we were told it would be fixed in November, then it got put back to December, again it got put back to January, now its nearly the end of Jan, I really cannot see how Virgin can promise speeds of 120Mb/s in Feb and with others being upgraded to 100Mb/s and 60Mb/s in this area.

Sort it out Virgin, actually upgrade your kit to handle it.

UPDATE - The fix is now not due until March

UPDATE 2 - 11/March = Surprise surprise its been put back until April
